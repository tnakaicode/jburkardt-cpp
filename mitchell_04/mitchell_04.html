mitchell_04
Peak Problem

mitchell_04, Mitchell's "peak" problem on the [0,+1]x[0,+1] square. Parameters XC, YC, and ALPHA may be varied.

  • XC = 0.5, YC = 0.5, ALPHA = 1000.
  • XC = 0.51, YC = 0.117, ALPHA = 100000.
