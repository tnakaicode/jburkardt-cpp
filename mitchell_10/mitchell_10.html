mitchell_10
Interior Line Singularity Problem

mitchell_10, Mitchell's "interior line singularity" problem on the [-1,+1]x[-1,+1] square. The parameters ALPHA and BETA may be varied.

  • ALPHA = 2.5, BETA = 0.0
  • ALPHA = 1.1, BETA = 0.0
  • ALPHA = 1.5, BETA = 0.6.
