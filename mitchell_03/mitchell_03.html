mitchell_03
Linear Elasticity

mitchell_03, Mitchell's "linear elasticity" problem, a coupled system of two equations with a mixed derivative in the coupling term, defined on the [-1,+1]x[-1,+1] square, with a slit from (0,0) to (1,0), and parameter values nu = 0.3, E = 1, and two parameters lambda and Q whose values may be varied:

  • lambda = 0.5444837367825, Q = 0.5430755788367.
  • lambda = 0.9085291898461, Q = -0.2189232362488.
