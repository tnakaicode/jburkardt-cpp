mitchell_02
Reentrant Corner

mitchell_02, Mitchell's "reentrant corner" problem, with the following variations for the value of the angle OMEGA:

  • 4 PI / 4 + 0.01
  • 5 PI / 4
  • 6 PI / 4
  • 7 PI / 4
  • 8 PI / 4.
