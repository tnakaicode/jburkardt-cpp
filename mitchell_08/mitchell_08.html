mitchell_08
Oscillatory Problem

mitchell_08, Mitchell's "oscillatory" problem on the [0,+1]x[0,+1] square. The parameter ALPHA may be varied.

  • ALPHA = 1 / ( 10 * PI ).
  • ALPHA = 1 / ( 50 * PI ).
